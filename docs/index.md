# Welcome to Phoenix's Fission

This is the official wiki for the **Phoenix's Fission** mod, a GTCEu addon focused on complex fission reactor mechanics.

### Features
- **Dynamic Heat Model**: Manage your reactor's temperature to optimize power and avoid meltdowns.
- **Multiblock Reactors**: Build large-scale fission reactors with customizable internal components.
- **Cooling Systems**: Utilize various coolers and coolants to keep your reactor safe.
- **KubeJS Integration**: Easily add custom fuel rods, moderators, and coolers.

### Getting Started
Check out the [Gameplay Guide](gameplay.md) to learn how to build your first reactor, or the [KubeJS Guide](kubejs.md) if you are a pack developer.
